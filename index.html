Brand Mission & Essence

How we empower clinical dermatological understanding before treatment begins.

"We empower individuals to understand their skin before starting any treatment. We do not sell treatments; we build clinical understanding. Education first. Treatment second."

Evidence-Based

Grounded in absolute clinical dermatological science. Every insight, guideline, and advice is verified by board-certified dermatologists.

Empathetic

A trusted, calm partner who listens without judgment. Validating skin struggles, showing real skin textures, and prioritizing barrier repair.

Culturally Nuanced

Calibrated specifically for the regional hot climate, UV index, and regional skin needs in Saudi Arabia, translating science into local context.

MIDGO Brand Identity Book 01.1 / Brand Foundation / Audience & Pain Points

Target Audience & Pain Points

Understanding skincare seekers in Saudi Arabia and their core frustrations.

Target Profile

Skincare enthusiasts, wellness seekers, and individuals dealing with chronic skin conditions in Saudi Arabia. Mostly young women aged 16–50 who value clinical clarity.

They seek dermatologist-backed insight rather than influencer trends, desiring to make smart decisions before purchasing cosmetics or clinical treatments.

Core Pain Points

1. Information Overload & Misinformation

Overwhelmed by cosmetic influencers promoting unverified, complex routines that harm the skin barrier.

2. Lack of Climate-Calibrated Advice

Dermatological resources calibrated for western environments, ignoring Saudi heat, UV index, and specific local skin issues.

3. High Cost of Trial and Error

Spending high amounts on cosmetic products without diagnostic certainty, triggering recurring barrier flareups.

MIDGO Brand Identity Book 01.2 / Brand Foundation / Brand Positioning

Brand Positioning & Promise

Defining MIDGO's unique role as a pre-treatment educational destination.

Brand Promise

We promise to transform complex dermatological pathology into digestible, evidence-based, and actionable digital knowledge. We provide the baseline understanding needed for healthy skin barriers, helping our community navigate skincare confidently and safely.

Positioning Statement

"Your First Destination to Understand Your Skin." MIDGO positions itself not as a treatment clinic or a cosmetic product house, but as a critical pre-treatment educational destination. We fill the educational gap, preparing patients to understand their needs before they buy products or consult doctors.
