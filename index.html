02

Education First. Treatment Second.

MIDGO is a digital dermatology education platform founded by a dermatologist. We empower people to understand their skin before starting any treatment. We do not sell treatments; we build understanding.

Our Mission

To democratize dermatological education by providing evidence-based, culturally nuanced, and scientifically rigorous guides that empower individuals in Saudi Arabia and the wider region.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital authority on skin wellness in the Middle East, transforming the way people approach skincare through research and interactive educational tools.

03

Brand Positioning

Bridging the gap between medical expertise and daily skincare practices.

Target Audience

Skincare enthusiasts, wellness seekers, and patients struggling with chronic skin conditions in Saudi Arabia who are overwhelmed by online misinformation and desire clinical clarity.

Brand Promise

We promise evidence-based, scientifically accurate dermatology knowledge translated into clear, actionable, and culturally relevant digital learning experiences.

AUDIENCE PAIN POINTS

Information Overload & Misinformation

+
Users are overwhelmed by beauty influencers promoting complex, unverified 10-step routines that often irritate the skin barrier.

Lack of Culturally Relevant Advice

+
Most dermatology literature is in English and calibrated for European/Western climates and skin types, ignoring regional heat, UV index, and specific local skin concerns.

High Cost of Trial-and-Error

+
Buying high-end skincare products blindly without knowing one's true skin barrier type leads to empty wallets and damaged skin.

Brand Motion

Visualizing transition speeds, motion physics, and user micro-interactions.

HOVER BOX TO TRIGGER TIMING MOTION

Easing physics: --ease-premium

Transitions on hover and click states use our custom easing property `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`. This results in an ultra-premium responsiveness where items accelerate instantly and decelerate gradually, reflecting human touch and biological softness.
